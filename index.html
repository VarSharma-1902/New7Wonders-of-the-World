New7Wonders of the World

The Swiss corporation New7Wonders Foundation started an initiative in 2001 to choose the New7Wonders of the World. This was done through online votes where over 200 selected monuments were to be voted for. The popularity poll via free Web-based voting and small amounts of telephone voting was led by Canadian-Swiss Bernard Weber and organized by the New 7 Wonders Foundation (N7W) based in Zurich, Switzerland.
